THE TEAM

The people
behind the work.

Founded in Singapore by Darryl and Devan on one belief: every business has a story worth telling, and deserves a website that tells it properly. Built from a blank canvas, for clients worldwide.

Since I was young, I've always had a heart for helping people. That drive led me to study Data Science, where I learned to use advanced technology to find insights that actually matter. Through the journey, I realized that for many businesses, technology feels cold or disconnected.

I started De Makers Space simply to help. I look for businesses that either have no digital presence or a website that isn't doing them justice, and I work to refine their brand so it truly reflects who they are.

My goal isn't just to hand over a project; it's to walk alongside my partners as an ally, providing the support and maintenance they need to grow through every milestone.

Darryl Founder + Visionary

With a background in Business and marketing experience across multiple industries, I've picked up one truth that never changes: the right depiction can transform a brand completely.

At De Makers Space, I'm the one asking the hard questions behind the scenes. Who is this business really speaking to? What do they want people to feel when they land on their site? And how do we make sure it actually converts? I work hand in hand with my partner to make sure every website we build isn't just beautiful. It's purposeful, and built to grow.

When I'm not looking at websites, you'll find me in the wrestling ring, where I've learned that energy, preparation, and showing up fully for your team is everything. That's exactly the kind of partner I am to every client we work with.

Devan Lead Strategist + Architect
