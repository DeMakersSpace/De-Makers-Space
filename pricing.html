  • Before any call, we personally build a Website Discovery Guide around your specific business — based on what you share in the contact form. It's a set of questions built around your business, your story, and what your website needs to do for you. Not a generic questionnaire. You fill it in at your own pace and send it back. It means that by the time we speak, we already understand your business deeply — so the conversation is meaningful, not introductory.

  • Our packages start from SGD $900 for a redesign, SGD $1,200 for a new Starter site, and SGD $2,800 for a Full Brand site. The final price is agreed on your discovery call based on exactly what your project needs — no surprises.

  • Your Starter and Full Brand packages include domain registration (first year) and hosting setup (first year). From year two, renewals are billed directly to you at cost, typically SGD $80–120/yr combined, depending on your domain and provider.

  • From discovery call to launch typically takes 3–6 weeks depending on the complexity of your site and how quickly we can work through feedback together. We'll give you a clear timeline on your discovery call.

  • Not technically. Your site will keep running. But maintenance gives you peace of mind, keeping your site secure, fast, and up-to-date as your business evolves. You can add it anytime, even after launch.

  • Yes. You can add pages, features, or the Ally Add-on at any point after launch. We'll quote any additions based on the scope of work. Think of your website as something that grows with your business.

  • Every site includes a solid SEO foundation — clean code, meta titles and descriptions, heading hierarchy, image alt text, and Google Search Console setup. The SEO + AEO Optimisation add-on takes this further: keyword research, full schema markup, and AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) with structured data and citation-ready content that gets your business recommended by AI tools like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity, not just ranked in traditional search.
